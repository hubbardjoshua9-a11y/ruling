Module 1 – Set up for safe, productive work

Before you touch a keyboard, two things make the rest of your work easier: understanding what you have actually been asked to do, and setting yourself up so you can work comfortably and safely. This module covers both.

In this module

  • 1.1 Understanding the task before you start
  • 1.2 Your workstation and your body
  • 1.3 Work health and safety for an office role

Assessment Readiness – Module 1

Before moving on, you should be able to:

  • read a task brief and identify its purpose, audience, format and presentation requirements
  • ask a clear, specific question to clarify a task when something is unclear
  • set up a workstation ergonomically (chair, monitor, keyboard and mouse, lighting)
  • describe a worker's basic work health and safety duties in an office role and explain how to report a hazard.

  • WorkSafe Victoria, Officewise: a guide to health and safety in the office: https://www.worksafe.vic.gov.au/resources/officewise-guide-health-and-safety-office-handbook
