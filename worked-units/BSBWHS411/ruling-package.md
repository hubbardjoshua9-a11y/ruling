# Worked unit — BSBWHS411 (mixed) · ADVISE rulings

**Implement and monitor WHS policies, procedures and programs** · the mixed case. Some of this unit must resist AI (consultation, hierarchy-of-control judgment, legislative accuracy); some has moved to AI-augmented (drafting WHS comms, analysing inspection and incident data). Warrant's split (`input-split.md`) makes the call element by element; Ruling acts on it. This is the unit that shows both axes firing in one package.

## The teaching point
This is the **mixed** unit. The currency axis fires REVISE-modernise on the four INTEGRATE tasks and **stays silent** on the RESIST ones (consultation, hierarchy-of-control judgment) — where the authenticity axis does the work instead. And note the contrast with BSBTEC201: WHS411's AI policy was **not** pre-built to allow task-level AI, so modernising the tasks also forces a **policy REVISE** (RULE-13). One package, both axes, and the policy interaction surfaced.
